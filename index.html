Davi Magalhães

WEB Developer

He was a fullstack programmer at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC), as a stable federal public servant, where he worked for 4 years developing internal management systems for departments, coordinations and management, using technologies such as Angular, Quasar and jQuery on the frontend and PHP pure and MariaDB on the backend.

For a period of one year, he worked, in parallel, providing support with computer formatting and software licenses, maintenance of network hacks, activation and maintenance of network points, passive network monitoring via Zabbix and user support via OTRS.

He also worked on the Continuing Training Platform of the Ministry of Education, through a project carried out by UFSC to MEC, budgeted at 6 million and with a team of 80 people, 5 of whom were 100% home office programmers for 2 years.

He has a degree in Software Engineering from the Federal University of Ceará, since 2018, where for 4 years he carried out research in embedded software, internet of things, prototyping in residential and industrial automation.

Since graduation, until now, he has studied development with agile and other methodologies; software documentation, UML modeling; software verification, validation and approval; tools, methods and processes focused on software quality.

Finally, he has been a web programmer since 2010, from the dark days when grids were made with tables.
