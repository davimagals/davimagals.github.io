Reservation of Physical Space

Project Date July 26th, 2019
Tags web, jquery, php
Client Federal University of Santa Catarina
Visit https://gestao.cfh.ufsc.br/reserva-externo

The Center for Philosophy and Human Sciences at the Federal University of Santa Catarina, responsible for 4 auditoriums, 2 halls and two rooms for events, offered to a community of approximately 4 thousand students and teachers, received around 20 reservation requests per day for these spaces, everything is done via email, which creates an overload for employees, requiring the dedicated work of at least one employee to respond to and manage all this demand.

With 6 different profiles of reservation requesters, with their own complex rules for event authorization and acceptance of reservations, the system now manages the entire request, authorization and notification process, no longer needing an employee just for this job.

In the images it is possible to see that in addition to the system implementation, all analysis and design was carried out following good process practices, using UML and ER modeling of class, activity, sequence and database diagrams.
